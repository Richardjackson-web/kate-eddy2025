A glimpse of our love story.

It all began on a rainy afternoon when Kate dashed into a small bookstore to escape the downpour, accidentally tripping over the threshold. Eddy, a photographer browsing a book on landscapes, noticed her stumble and handed her umbrella back with a teasing smile. That small exchange turned into a conversation, and before they knew it, hours had passed as they bonded over books, dreams, and their shared love for life’s quiet moments.


Months later, Eddy took Kate to a secluded beach at sunset, where the golden sky mirrored the warmth of their connection. With the sound of waves crashing behind them, he got down on one knee and asked her to be his forever. Through happy tears, Kate said yes, and now, as they prepare to walk down the aisle, their love story continues—simple, beautiful, and destined.
